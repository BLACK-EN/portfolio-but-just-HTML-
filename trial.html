WORK & RESUME

I had an engineering diploma in instrumentation and electronics from INSAT in Tunisia in 2015 .

I had a Master in embedded systems from UPJV in France in 2016 .

I also got a certificate in Cryptography from Stanford University in 2017 .

I worked on the QB50 project (50 cubesats launched in 2016-2017) with INSSET .

I worked on UPJV during her graduation project .

Among the spacecrafts that I worked in: Jupiter Icy Moons Satellite, Biomass satellite, Met-Op C satellite, En-Map satellite and a non ESA project: Kompsat7 satellite (South Korean).
